EVOX provides local numbers throughout Taiwan

No matter where your headquarters or branches are physically located, EVOX can provide local numbers for any area in Taiwan. With EVOX, calls to any of these numbers can be answered and managed by the same auto-attendant or live receptionist. You can even set up a virtual branch to have a local presence without having a physical office. The phone systems for all your branches are managed by a single admin website.

Choose different devices for each location

The personnel at some locations may not necessarily have an office, or they are usually on the go. Depending on the situation, your staff can choose the most suitable smartphone or PC/Mac device to make and receive calls. Setting up a new branch no longer requires a physical office or equipment. EVOX keeps all your devices and branches connected as if they are in the same location.

Customize your phone system for every branch

Each branch can be configured so that a single member or a group of members can answer incoming calls. With group ring, you can make sure important customers reach a team member that can assist them. In future releases, the phone system for each branch can be customized with its own auto attendant and user and extension settings.
