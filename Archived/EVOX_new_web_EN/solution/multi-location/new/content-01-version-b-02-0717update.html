提供全台各地區域號碼

無論你的總部、分公司、據點位於台灣哪個縣市，EVOX皆能提供在地區域的電話號碼，而且，還能讓分處於各地的據點或分店皆透過同一個自動總機或真人專員來接聽來電。即使在某些地區尚未有辦公室，也能擁有當地電話號碼作為公司服務號碼。

Free calls between branch offices

Employees in HQ or branch locations can reach each other free of charge by dialing the extension number or clicking an extension in the contact list. Adding new branches is easy and free. Just add a branch in the HQ admin site and start calling.

Flexible and efficient management​

Managing communications systems for multiple locations has never been easier. The HQ administrator can quickly configure multiple branch accounts and monitor their call detail records. While subscription management and payment are centralized, local Branch administrators can be authorized to customize the auto-attendant, add/delete users, and assign resources to users for their branches.​
