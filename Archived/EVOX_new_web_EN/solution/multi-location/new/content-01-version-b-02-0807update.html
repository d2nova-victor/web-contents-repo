EVOX provides local numbers throughout Taiwan

No matter where your headquarters or branches are physically located, EVOX can provide local numbers for any area in Taiwan. With EVOX, calls to any of these numbers can be answered and managed by the same auto-attendant or live receptionist.

Free calls between branch offices

Employees in HQ or branch locations can reach each other free of charge by dialing the extension number or clicking an extension in the contact list. Adding new branches is easy and free. Just add a branch in the HQ admin site and start calling.

Flexible and efficient management​

Managing communications systems for multiple locations has never been easier. The HQ administrator can quickly configure multiple branch accounts and monitor their call detail records. While subscription management and payment are centralized, local Branch administrators can be authorized to customize the auto-attendant, add/delete users, and assign resources to users for their branches.​

Choose different devices for each location

The personnel at some locations may not necessarily have an office, or they are usually on the go. Depending on the situation, your staff can choose the most suitable smartphone or PC/Mac device to make and receive calls. Setting up a new branch no longer requires a physical office or equipment. EVOX keeps all your devices and branches connected as if they are in the same location.
