Powerful yet intuitive mobile extension

The right tools are a prerequisite to successful execution. EVOX mobile and PC apps allow employees to work where and how they choose. For businesses that require frequent customer visits for sales or support, their outside team can easily contact customers, stay in touch with their home office, and never miss an important call. In addition, EVOX provides a user-friendly interface for easy and intuitive operation, and allows users to separate their business and personal calls and contacts.
