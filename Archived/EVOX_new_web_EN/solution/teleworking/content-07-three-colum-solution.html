Telecommute with EVOX

Take business calls to your office from home

With EVOX mobile extensions, you will receive calls to your company number on your mobile phone or PC app (or IP phone). Customers don’t need to know your personal mobile or home phone when they want to contact your company.

Make business calls from your home as if you are in the office

When you make a call from your EVOX mobile or PC app (or IP phone) only your company number will show up on the other side.This gives you a professional look, and your customers will not know you are calling from home or a coffee shop.

Remote management

EVOX operates on the AWS cloud and requires no hardware. It can be managed anywhere from a PC with the EVOX Admin website. This ability to manage users and reprogram the Auto-attendant or work hours on the fly totally disrupts the legacy PBX experience.
