The cloud is everywhere, so you are connected everywhere

Cloud PBX removes the distance between your Hong Kong office and overseas staff and improves inter-office collaboration. For example, when your business encounters a customer issue in Hong Kong, a specialist in Taiwan can be connected with a few clicks on the EVOX app. Or, if your Hong Kong office needs to communicate with a colleague in an overseas office, they can just call him on his extension as if he is just down the hall.
