How an ACD helps to manage customer calls

  • An ACD can be designed to improve the responsiveness of your contact center to customer calls in a number of ways.

  • By using a front end IVR (Interactive Voice Response) menu, the system can query the caller to find out how to best direct the call.

  • Callers with a routine request or questions are directed to a pre-recorded message. Callers that require more personal care are directed to the right agent or team to resolve their issue.
