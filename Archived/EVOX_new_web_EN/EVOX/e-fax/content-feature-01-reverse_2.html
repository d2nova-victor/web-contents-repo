EVOX Fax

EVOX Fax is a digital Fax service. To send a Fax, create a PDF and email it the EVOX Fax service. When you receive it Fax, it will arrive as a PDF attachment to an email. This allows you to send or receive Fax no matter where you are, using your PC or mobile phone. No more trips to the convenience store or to the office just to send a Fax!
