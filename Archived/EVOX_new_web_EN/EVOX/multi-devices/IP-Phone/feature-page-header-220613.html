IP Phones for EVOX

For users who want to have desk phones or conference room phones, EVOX works with all major brands of IP Phones. IP phones purchased from EVOX are pre-configured by EVOX.If you wish to purchase IP phone(s) from another vendor, view thecompatibility list. Once you have purchased compatible models, contact EVOX support; a setup fee will be charged to register the IP phone(s) to your account.
