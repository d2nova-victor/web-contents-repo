How EVOX works with an IP Door Phone

  • Step 1：When someone rings the doorbell, the system will automatically call the operator’s EVOX App and display IP Door Phone on their caller ID.
  • Step 2：Answering the call enables two-way communication between the visitor and operator.
  • Step 3：If appropriate, the operator can unlock the door by entering the password on the EVOX App.​ ​
