What is a Cloud PBX?

Provides all of the functions of legacy PBX for your office's internal and external voice calling. Rich features include auto-attendant with time sensitive company greetings, call hold, transfer, 3 way calls, and call pick up. Cloud PBX offers additional features such as company number and extensions, multi-location services, low cost calling, and digital fax. With no hardware to purchase, install, or maintain, cloud PBX upgrades and grows with your business.
