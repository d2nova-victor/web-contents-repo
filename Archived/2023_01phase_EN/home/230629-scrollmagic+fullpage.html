Reduce carbon emissions

Running EVOX on AWS effectively reduces carbon emissions by up to 90%.

A company using a 350W small/medium IP PBX, 100 IP telephones and a 100 square foot air conditioned machine room generates about 26,718 kilowatt-hours of electricity a year. This is equivalent to 13.6 metric tons of CO2 emissions. With EVOX, a similarly sized cloud system only emits 1.36 metric tons of CO2 emissions per year.

Secure your information

Information security and protection

  • EVOX’s core network is located in AWS, protected by SBC.
  • The EVOX App uses OAuth 2.0 and 2-factor to authenticate.

Information security certification

  • EVOX passed the Taiwan Industry Bureau’s Mobile Information Security (MAS) alliance certification against vulnerability scanning and penetration testing.

Personal data protection

  • EVOX complies with European GDPR and Taiwan’s personal information protection laws.

Anti-spam
