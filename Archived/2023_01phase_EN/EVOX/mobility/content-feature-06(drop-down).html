All the office phone functions and more

The mobile app supports basic functions including dialing out, answering calls, transfer, and hold/resume.

You can customize call forwarding to never, always, or for when you are busy. The call forward destination can be assigned to an extension, another phone number, or the Auto-attendant.

When activated, you will be notified of calls to your colleagues so you can pick up the call when they are busy or not available.
