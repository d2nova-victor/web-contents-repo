EVOX Fax

EVOX lets you send and receive faxes digitally with no need for a fax machine. To send a fax, create a PDF and email it to the fax service. When you receive a fax it will arrive as a PDF attachment to an email. This allows you to send and receive company faxes from your PC or mobile phone from anywhere.

  • This value-added service comes with a dedicated phone number for faxing。
