詳洽業務

Customize your contact center according to your needs.

EVOX Connect products include Connect ACD, Connect App, Connect Manager, and Connect Database. As your company grows, your EVOX Connect system can expand by adding new services.

Connect Automatic Call Distribution (ACD)

With an inbound ACD, you can create custom interactive voice response systems with bypass options, call queuing with the option to leave voice messages for call back, and design call distribution algorithms.

Includes one DID number, with the option to purchase additional DIDs as needed

includes 4 channels for each DID with options for additional channels depending on the number of agents and length of call queues.

Can be converted from existing EVOX users or new users, and organized into teams and scheduled for one or more ACDs.

Connect Manager

Automatic call distribution, interactive voice recognition, and other company settings can be flexibly managed at any time from a browser window.

Customer service management allows service centers to efficiently allocate personnel time and improve the customer experience.

Voice recording and transcript are directly accessible for review, edit, and analysis. They can also be downloaded.

Available in the next release to allow EVOX Connect managers to customize the database according to business needs. (Currently this can be done using the Connect App.)

Onboarding Consulting Services

Subscription of EVOX Connect ACD includes consulting services to help you design and program ACD(s) and organize teams for each ACD. Initial users of Connect App will also be provided with special engineering support to create and customize the Contact Knowledge database and Connect App relationship information.

Connect App

An upgraded PC/Mac app that unlocks features to incorporate contact knowledge into the EVOX system. The Connect App can be subscribed by EVOX Connect agents, or EVOX users when an ACD is not required.

Contact information and knowledge sharing

Generate and manage call notes, memos, and tasks during or after a call (as part of the contact knowledge base)

Agents assigned to an ACD may view work schedule, and assign voice messages to other agents and/or users for collaboration.

With voice recording option, each user/agent can view and edit voice recording and transcript.

Connect Knowledge Database

Provide a better customer service experience and increase agent and user collaboration with a customizable database of contact information, contact history, and other knowledge.

* The current release of EVOX Connect provides tools to create and manage the database using the Connect App. The next release of Connect Manager will include tools to create and customize the database.
